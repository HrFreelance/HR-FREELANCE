## Hello

I'm Janardhan Bathula — HR Executive based in Visakhapatnam, currently managing the complete HR function for 60+ employees at Kankatala Textiles, and pursuing my MBA in HR Management.

My career has moved between two very different HR environments: **US IT staffing** (where speed, precision, and candidate psychology are everything) and **manufacturing HR** (where compliance, culture, and operational discipline dominate). That contrast has given me a wider perspective than most HR professionals my age, and I want to make it useful.

---

## What You'll Find Here

### Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

Real sourcing strategies, AI-assisted workflows (I hold the LinkedIn GenAI certification), Boolean search construction, and how to manage high-volume hiring without sacrificing quality or candidate experience.

### Payroll & Statutory Compliance

India's compliance landscape — PF, ESI, PT, TDS — is genuinely complex, and the cost of getting it wrong is real. I'll share practical guides, checklists, and the templates I use every month.

### Employee Engagement & Retention

Not the fluffy stuff. The five practices that actually show up in attrition numbers. How to make grievance handling work. Why most recognition programs fail.

### HR Analytics

How to turn GreytHR data, attendance records, and recruitment funnels into insights that actually change management decisions. No data science degree required.

### Career Perspectives

Building an HR career in India, the MBA experience at Srinivasa Institute, and what I've learned working across two very different industry sectors.
